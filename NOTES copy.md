## Overview of Ojasen:

Capabilities of the system:

- Customer makes inquiry about services
- Ojasen sends link to acquire customer details
- Customer inputs customer details into online form
- Ojasen gets notified of details and sends invoices (paper and e-invoice)
- Customer makes payment
- Ojasen assigns a facilitator to the customer
- Facilitator has service fees
- Customer is charged based on the total fee structure (ojasen + facilitator)
- Ojasen can see total revenue from customer and facilitator
- Ojasen acan see total history of customer across multiple seasons
- Revenue/Profit over time (based on pricing and fee structure)
- Loyalty/Voucher/etc + Yatesh can track campaigns
- Google Analytics and data driven insights
- Can add events to their personal calendar

CRM + ERD + Accounting
